# New Business Sales skills

This directory contains skills used by the New Business Sales team (specifically Technical Account Executives or TAEs) to qualify, evaluate, and outreach to various types of leads.

## Available Skills

*   **[posthog-inbound-lead](./posthog-inbound-lead)**: Evaluates inbound leads from Salesforce. Maps the lead to one of PostHog's core use cases, checks Vitally for existing account context, recommends a disposition (e.g., qualify for call, route to self-serve), and drafts a tailored response email.
*   **[posthog-pls-big-fish](./posthog-pls-big-fish)**: Researches and qualifies "big fish" product-led leads (large companies with 500+ employees using PostHog on the free tier). Focuses on analyzing Vitally user activity to recommend whether to skip, nurture, or engage with a support-first outreach.
*   **[posthog-pls-transition-leads](./posthog-pls-transition-leads)**: Qualifies product-led leads approaching a billing transition (e.g., startup program customers rolling off their $50K credits, or accounts about to hit a high first invoice >=$2K). Analyzes billing data to provide cost optimization recommendations alongside targeted outreach.
